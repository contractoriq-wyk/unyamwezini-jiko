# Unyamwezini Jiko La Mama JJJ 🔥

**Authentic Tanzanian Soul Food · Columbia, Maryland**

Website for Unyamwezini Jiko La Mama JJJ — a licensed Maryland Cottage Food business
specializing in authentic Tanzanian baked goods: Maandazi, Chapati, Vitumbua, and Boflo Rolls.

- Maryland Cottage Food legal compliance
